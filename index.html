About

Romania-born poet and author whose work centers on faith, prayer, and hope through life’s trials.

Short bio

Mihaela S. Hegstrom is a Romania-born Christian poet and author whose writing invites readers to reflect on God’s guidance through seasons of hardship and renewal. Trained in engineering and education, she moved with her family to the Chicago area in 1979. Her poetry collections explore praise, prayer, grace, and truth with a steady voice meant to encourage.
